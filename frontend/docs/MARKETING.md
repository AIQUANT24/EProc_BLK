## 1. Brand Identity

### Brand Name
**NMICOV** — National Make-in-India Compliance & Origin Verification Ledger

### Tagline
**"Verify. Trust. Procure."**

### Brand Promise
Every rupee of government procurement genuinely supports domestic manufacturing.

### Brand Values
1. **Transparency** — Open, verifiable compliance records
2. **Trust** — Blockchain-backed immutable proof
3. **Efficiency** — Automated verification replacing manual processes
4. **Scale** — Built for India's entire procurement ecosystem
5. **Innovation** — AI + Blockchain for next-gen compliance

## 2. Key Messages

### For Government Decision-Makers
> "NMICOV transforms PPP-MII compliance from a manual paper exercise into automated, blockchain-verified digital infrastructure — saving time, preventing fraud, and strengthening domestic manufacturing."

### For Suppliers/Manufacturers
> "Get your Make in India compliance certified automatically. Register your products, declare your BOM, and receive a blockchain-backed compliance certificate — free for small businesses."

### For Procurement Officers
> "Verify any supplier's domestic content claim in seconds. NMICOV gives you data-backed confidence with DVA scores, fraud detection, and immutable audit trails."

### For Media/Public
> "India's public procurement spends ₹88 lakh crore annually. NMICOV ensures every rupee genuinely supports Indian manufacturing through automated verification and blockchain proof."

## 4. Launch Campaign

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (4 weeks before)
- Teaser campaign: "Something is changing in Indian procurement"
- Stakeholder briefings (DPIIT, GeM, CII)
- Press kit preparation
- Beta invite waitlist

### Phase 2: Launch Week
- Press release to Economic Times, Mint, Business Standard
- LinkedIn announcement with explainer video
- Government stakeholder launch event
- Live demo webinar (3 sessions: Hindi, English, industry-specific)

### Phase 3: Post-Launch (8 weeks)
- Supplier onboarding campaign (GeM seller community)
- Regional workshops (10 major cities)
- Case study: first 100 compliance verifications
- User testimonials and success stories
